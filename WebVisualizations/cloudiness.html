Latitude vs. Cloudiness %

Analysis: The City Latitude vs. Cloudiness graphs shows that there is no correlation between a city's latitude and it's cloud level. This observation is expected, as a city's cloud level is determined by location, not on a map, but location/proximity to natural formations, such as mountains and water bodies.
